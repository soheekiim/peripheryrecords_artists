NO VACATION

No Vacation is an American dream pop band from San Francisco, California, currently based in Brooklyn, New York. Formed in 2015, the group consists of interchanging members. Current/previous members include Sabrina Mai, Marisa Saunders, Nat Lee, Rob Mills, and Harrison Spencer. No Vacation is known for their nostalgic, surf-pop sound.
