THE PEACHSKINS

The Peachskins is an indie synthpop band based in Malaysia. The band is composed of frontman Rafique Ramleh, drummer Sadman Yeasir and bassist / synths Arnold Tham. Since 2017, the Peachskins has grown its musical style from the indie synth pop sound to a more mature R&B experience.
