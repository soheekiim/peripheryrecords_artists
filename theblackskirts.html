THE BLACK SKIRTS

The Black Skirts is an indie rock one-man band based in South Korea. Founded by Korean-American musician Bryan Cho, he alone is the vocalist, guitarist, bassist, keyboardist, and percussionist of the band. Since debut, the Black Skirts has won 'Best Modern Rock Album' twice in Korea.
