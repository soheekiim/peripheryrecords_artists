THE RADIO DEPT.

The Radio Dept. is a dream pop band based in Sweden composed of members Johan Duncanson, Martin Larsson, and Daniel Tjader. Their music includes genres such as alternative rock, shoegazing, and twee pop. The band has always been a very politically charged band and several releases with sharp political commentary have emerged throughout the years. The Radio Dept. has been active since 2001.
